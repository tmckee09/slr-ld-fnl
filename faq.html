Frequently Asked Questions

What does "$0 out-of-pocket" really mean?

You won’t pay upfront for your solar installation. The program is designed to offer affordable options that help you reduce your energy bill from day one, without large initial costs.

Do I qualify for solar?

If you’re a homeowner with a decent roof and a good amount of sun exposure, you likely qualify. The free consultation will confirm this with no pressure or obligation.

How much can I save?

Many homeowners save up to 40% on their electric bill. Actual savings vary based on your location, usage, and chosen plan.

Who installs the system?

All installations are completed by certified, licensed solar installers local to your area.

What if I sell my home?

Solar can increase your home’s value. In many cases, the solar setup can be transferred to the new homeowner or resolved during the sale process with ease.
